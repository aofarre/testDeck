Commercial Energy Rates App

Bubble plot and geo-visualization of of USA energy rates

Anthony O'Farrell
Developing Data Products - Coursera Project

USA Energy

  1. USA has a largely deregulated energy market.
  2. Commercial energy rates can vary substantially from state to state.
  3. The US Energy Information Administration (EIA) collects lots of useful data and makes it publicly available.

Energy Information Administration (EIA)

  1. The EIA website (www.eia.gov) is a rich resource for energy information.
  2. It is updated on a regular basis.
  3. It is the go to site for anything energy related.

Commercial Energy Rates

  1. Commercial energy rates vary from state to state.
  2. The EIA collects data on commercial energy rates for every state.
  3. The data on commercial enery rates is updated monthly.

What the App does

  1. Scrapes commercial ernergy rate data from the EIA site.
  2. Scrapes state population data from the US Census site.
  3. The controls allow the user to filter out some of the data.
  4. The end result is a data visualization that takes useful publicly availabale energy data and delivers insight to an end user that might not be immediately apparent from the table of data provided by EIA.
